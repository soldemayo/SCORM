We cannot forget the Fraud and Quality subprocesses in the management of recovery case files; although it is true that they are not specific processes in this typology, they must be considered as indicated in the Claims Operating Model.

As regards fraud management, it is usually identified in the associated main case file.

With regard to the quality subprocess, we must emphasize management in the recovery of deductibles as a primary issue, since it is these that will have the most impact on our clients.

Technological tools should support the handling of this type of case file, unifying management criteria and deadlines. One of the most important features is the claims handling plan, which makes it possible to identify in an orderly manner the actions to be carried out by the handlers in accordance with the quality and efficiency standards set out in the specific model.

Some of the unique features for Recovery management include:

The process of capturing and opening recovery case files encompasses the set of actions aimed at obtaining and recording information related to a potential recovery.

The conventional recovery handling process comprises the activities to validate whether the conventional recovery case file is appropriate and to begin its management in order for MAPFRE to recover the amount that it has previously paid to its policyholder under the current policy and that it recovers from the third party responsible for an accident or claim (or from its insurer).

The agreement recovery handling process comprises the activities to validate, through agreements that can be established at the sector, bilateral or multilateral level between insurance companies, whether or not the recovery case files are appropriate. Their scope of action and content will depend on each agreement.

The insurers' right of recourse is configured as a power to recover from the insured party what has been previously paid to the injured party in the cases determined by law or as the result of an agreement between the parties. The Right of Recourse is closely linked to the local legislation and, consequently, it should always be taken into consideration before initiating any process.

The collections and payments process for recovery case files covers the activities related to the management of the recovery and the possible issuing of any payment.

The recovery closing process covers the activities necessary for the closing or reopening of recovery case files (conventional and agreement) and recourse recoveries.

The monitoring and supervision of recoveries includes follow-up and monitoring activities at the level of the recovery case file, as well as from a global perspective of the status of all recoveries. It includes the planning and definition of objectives, the analysis and monitoring of critical control points and commitments made to third parties, as well as the implementation of monitoring actions.
